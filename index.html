Greta in a nutshell

I am a software engineer, cellist, triathlete, and climber.

I have spent most of my life as a cellist, earning various degrees in the field and contributing to my local community through teaching and performing.

Currently, I am most interested in the world of computer programming and systems architecture. I enjoy working with C#, Python, and Javascript, as well as AWS services and Linux. I would also like to learn more about OpenStack, blockchain, and information security.
